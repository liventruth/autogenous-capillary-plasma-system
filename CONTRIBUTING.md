Contributing to the ACPS Project

We welcome contributions from fluid dynamicists, material scientists, and firmware engineers to refine this public utility.

How to Contribute

Identify a Bounty: Review the active engineering challenges in our repository's Issues tab.

Fork the Repository: Create a copy of this architecture to your own GitHub account to make changes.

Submit a Pull Request: Submit your mathematical models, G-code updates, or lab validation data back to the main branch for peer review.

Licensing & Compliance

By contributing to this repository, you agree that your designs, code, firmware, and modifications will be released under the same CERN-OHL-S license, keeping this technology free, open, and accessible to humanity forever.

Any derivatives of this physical hardware design must also be open-sourced under the exact same terms.
